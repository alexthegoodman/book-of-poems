Form Unions

Be not deceived by decoys

Do not let a disguise fool you

Allah's true marriage is holy

Earth has not made anything better

Its laws uphold what they cannot

Indeed, across all seas and continents

The people form their unions

Allah and His Angels record it not

Better it is for people not to swear

Let them not make promises

Make no oaths, write no vows

The false marriages are mere legal bonds

They are left broken and trampled

Or respected as a mere arrangement

Allah's true marriage is love

A bond which cannot break

Known by unconditional love

Be of the knowledgeable

Have Allah's blessing for you

Earthly unions may help people

But they desecrate true marriage

The children deserve a father and a mother

But let them call it a union rather than marriage

For the union they are permitted to break

But a true marriage would keep them tied

Full of animosity and friction

Like a trap rather than a miracle
