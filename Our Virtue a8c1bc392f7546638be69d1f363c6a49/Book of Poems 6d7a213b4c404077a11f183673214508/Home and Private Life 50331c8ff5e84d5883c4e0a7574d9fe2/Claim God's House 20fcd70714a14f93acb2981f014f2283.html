Claim God's House

The owner of the house

Carries the blame and its burdens

But a homeowner should be reliable

Leaders are disgusted by neglect

They care for their people dearly

And ensure their protection

Let them fill their house with light

Calling their Creator to be the Head

Declaring their house God's House

A house abiding by God's Law

Spared of sin and its destruction

Give the high and the low equal time

Treat the big and small as equals

Love the whole of the house

Thereafter love each of its people

The authority of your people

Best seeks the benefit of them

Supporting Verses

  • Seek the greater love, to care not for one person, but for the whole house
  • This is My House, so I declare My House a House of Allah, and within its walls there shall not be sin, so that the People and spirits within shall be spared all sorts of destruction
  • The High and The Low are treated equitably in this house because this is a House of Allah
  • Whatever is yours is yours to direct, and a House of Allah shall follow the Law of Allah, so if that house is yours then it will be yours to direct, yet if you are likened unto an authority, then seek the benefit of your People
