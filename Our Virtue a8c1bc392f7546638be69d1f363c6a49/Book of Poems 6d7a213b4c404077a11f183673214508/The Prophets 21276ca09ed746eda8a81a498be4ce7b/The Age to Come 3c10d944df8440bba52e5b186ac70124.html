The Age to Come

The modern world is a woeful one

It’s people care not for each other,

They forgive only little or in return

The people of the modern world love themselves

They act in mobs and subjugate people heartlessly

Though God’s Children always speak up against heinous deeds

The overpaid subjugate the underpaid

The strong subjugate the abused

The healthy waste away the sick joyfully

Yet the current affair is only a passing time

The coming generations will bring about the Coming Age

Peace will fill our homes

We will have peace at our tables

Love will be in our air

For the people abhor their lost time

When they used to ridicule each other

Thanks to their unmovable inner pains

Streets will be paved with gold

Not for a love of wealth or extravagance

But in honor of our Loving Creator

Earth will cool pleasantly and restore itself

The animals will be grateful and multiply

All the people will enjoy quiet paradise

Culture will have no power on the whole Earth

And the least among us will be the greatest

For the whole people have faith in Jesus Christ

The people will even desire Godly things

They will pray for virtues and for others

They will hope to be generous and wish to be taught
